# Support

**Kaumei&nbsp;JDBC** is maintained by a single person in personal time.
Support is best-effort only.

---

## Where to ask

- **GitHub Issues:** reproducible bugs and concrete tasks  
- **GitHub Discussions:** questions, ideas, and design proposals  

Please keep requests focused and technical.
Unclear or very broad topics may be closed or redirected.

---

## What to expect

- There is no guaranteed response time.
- Not every request will receive a reply.
- Not every request will be accepted or implemented.

---

## Scope

Support is limited to **Kaumei&nbsp;JDBC** itself and documented behaviour.
It does not include production support, private consulting, or general JDBC education.

---

If you use **Kaumei&nbsp;JDBC** in production, you are responsible for validation,
monitoring, and risk management.
